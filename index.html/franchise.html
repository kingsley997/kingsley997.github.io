Our Franchise Application Process

We've streamlined our process to make it clear and efficient for prospective partners.

  1. Inquiry & Initial Contact: Fill out our online inquiry form or contact us directly. We'll send you an information packet.
  2. Discovery Call: A representative will connect with you to discuss your background, goals, and answer initial questions.
  3. Application & Due Diligence: Submit a detailed application. We'll conduct necessary checks and provide you with our Franchise Disclosure Document (FDD).
  4. Meet the Team: An opportunity to meet our leadership team and get a deeper understanding of our vision and support system.
  5. Franchise Agreement: Upon mutual agreement, we sign the franchise agreement, making you an official Alpha Abacus partner.
  6. Training & Setup: Begin comprehensive training, receive support for setting up your center, and prepare for your grand opening!

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the initial investment?

The initial investment varies depending on location and center size. We will provide detailed financial information in our FDD.

Do I need an educational background?

While an educational background is a plus, it's not strictly required. We look for passionate individuals with strong business acumen.

What kind of training is provided?

We provide extensive training covering curriculum, teaching methodologies, operational procedures, and marketing strategies.

How long does the setup process take?

Typically, the setup process from signing to opening takes between 3-6 months, depending on various factors.
