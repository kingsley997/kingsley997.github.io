Basic Program: Building the Foundation

Who is this program for?

Designed for children aged 5-7 years old who are new to abacus or early learners in mathematics. No prior abacus experience is required.

What will your child learn?

  • Abacus Introduction: Parts of the abacus and basic finger movements.
  • Number Recognition: Representing numbers on the abacus.
  • Simple Operations: Addition and subtraction (single and double digits).
  • Mental Math Basics: Introduction to visualizing the abacus for mental calculations.
  • Focus & Concentration: Early development of attention span and listening skills.

Intermediate Program: Mastering Calculations

Who is this program for?

Ideal for children aged 7-9 years old who have completed our Basic Program or have a foundational understanding of abacus addition and subtraction.

What will your child learn?

  • Multiplication & Division: Techniques for using the abacus for complex operations.
  • Decimal Operations: Introduction to decimal point calculations.
  • Speed Training: Exercises to improve calculation speed and accuracy.
  • Mental Arithmetic Practice: Enhanced visualization and mental calculation without the physical abacus.
  • Memory & Retention: Activities designed to strengthen memory recall.

Advanced Program: Unleashing Mental Agility

Who is this program for?

For children aged 9 years and above who have completed our Intermediate Program or demonstrate strong proficiency in abacus operations.

What will your child learn?

  • Mental Arithmetic Mastery: Solving complex calculations purely mentally at high speed.
  • Square Roots & Cube Roots: Advanced operations using mental abacus.
  • Logical Reasoning: Strengthening analytical and problem-solving skills.
  • Concentration & Focus: Deepening the ability to maintain intense concentration.
  • Confidence Boost: Achieving exceptional mental math abilities builds immense self-esteem.
