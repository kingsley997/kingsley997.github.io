About Alpha Abacus

Our journey to revolutionize mental arithmetic education.

Our Story

Alpha Abacus was founded in 2010 with a vision to make mental math accessible and enjoyable for children. Frustrated by traditional rote learning methods, our founders, experienced educators and mathematicians, developed a curriculum that combines ancient abacus techniques with modern pedagogical approaches. What started as a small local center quickly grew into a nationwide network, empowering thousands of children to unlock their cognitive potential and build lifelong skills.

We believe that every child possesses unique abilities waiting to be discovered. Our commitment to innovation, quality, and student-centric learning has made us a trusted name in abacus education.

Our Teaching Philosophy

At Alpha Abacus, our philosophy centers on holistic child development. We go beyond just teaching calculations; we focus on building a strong foundation for cognitive growth. Our approach combines:

  • Interactive Learning: Engaging activities and games make learning fun.
  • Personalized Attention: Small class sizes ensure individual progress.
  • Experienced Educators: Certified teachers passionate about child development.
  • Progressive Curriculum: Step-by-step mastery, from basics to advanced mental math.
  • Positive Reinforcement: Encouraging environment to boost confidence.

We believe that a joyful learning environment leads to the best outcomes. Our philosophy nurtures not just sharp minds, but well-rounded, confident individuals.
